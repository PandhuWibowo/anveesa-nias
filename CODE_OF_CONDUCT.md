# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Anveesa Nias to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

Participants are expected to communicate professionally, assume good intent, and focus critique on ideas, code, design, and project outcomes.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Accept maintainers' decisions when they are made to protect quality, safety, or scope.
- Credit others' work where appropriate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated hostile comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or disruptive behavior.
- Any conduct that would make reasonable contributors feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants when behavior violates this code of conduct.

If you need to report conduct concerns, use the repository's private contact channel or GitHub's reporting tools. Include context, links, screenshots, and dates where possible.
